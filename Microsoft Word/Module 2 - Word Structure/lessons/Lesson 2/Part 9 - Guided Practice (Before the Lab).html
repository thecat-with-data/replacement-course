Part 9 - Guided Practice (Before the Lab)

Before starting W-Lab 3 , practice the following:

  1. Create a bulleted list of five items
  2. Convert it to a numbered list
  3. Add two sub-points under one item
  4. Promote and demote items using Indent controls
  5. Restart numbering in a new section
  6. Change bullet or number style while preserving structure
