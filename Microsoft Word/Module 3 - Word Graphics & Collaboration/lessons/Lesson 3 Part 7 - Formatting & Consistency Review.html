Lesson 3 Part 7 - Formatting & Consistency Review

Perform a final scan for consistency.

Check:

  • Font type and size
  • Line spacing
  • Paragraph spacing
  • Heading alignment
  • Table and figure formatting

Be alert for:

  • Random spacing
  • Inconsistent styles
  • Misaligned visuals
  • Text wrapping errors

Consistency is one of the strongest indicators of a professionally prepared document.
