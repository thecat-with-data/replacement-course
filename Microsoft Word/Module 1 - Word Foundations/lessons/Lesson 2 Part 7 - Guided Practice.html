Lesson 2 Part 7 - Guided Practice

Do this before attempting the lab to strengthen your skills.

  1. Write two paragraphs of text, or use the passage above.
    • Make the first left-aligned with 1.15 spacing.
    • Make the second justified with 6-pt spacing before/after.
  2. Add a First Line Indent to both paragraphs.
  3. Create:
    • A bulleted list of your hobbies
    • A numbered list of five steps for making coffee.
    • A multilevel list breaking down a topic of your choice.
  4. Add a highlighted term and practice removing the highlight.
  5. Use Format Painter to copy the style from one paragraph to another.
