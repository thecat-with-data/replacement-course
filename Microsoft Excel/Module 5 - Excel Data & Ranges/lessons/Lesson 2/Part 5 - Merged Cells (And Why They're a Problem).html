Part 5: Merged Cells (And Why They're a Problem)

Merged cells are one of the most common Excel mistakes.

Problems with Merged Cells

  • Break sorting and filtering
  • Prevent proper use of formulas
  • Cause issues with tables and charts
  • Lead to inconsistent formatting
  • Confuse screen readers

Better Alternatives

  • Center Across Selection
  • Formatting headers without merging
  • Using titles above the dataset

Rule:

Do not merge cells inside data ranges.
