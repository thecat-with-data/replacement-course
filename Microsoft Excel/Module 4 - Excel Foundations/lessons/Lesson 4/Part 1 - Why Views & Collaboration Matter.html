Part 1 - Why Views & Collaboration Matter

Excel is not just a calculation tool—it is a workspace.

Professionals often work with:

  • Large datasets
  • Shared workbooks
  • Multiple contributors
  • Print-ready outputs

If views are not configured intentionally:

  • Headers disappear when scrolling
  • Important data is misread
  • Printouts become unusable
  • Collaborators struggle to understand the file

This lesson teaches you how to control your Excel environment and prepare data for others.
