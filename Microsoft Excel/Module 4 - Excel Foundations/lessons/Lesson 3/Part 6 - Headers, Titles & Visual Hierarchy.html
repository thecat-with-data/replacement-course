Part 6 - Headers, Titles & Visual Hierarchy

Excel does not automatically "know" what is a header.

Header Row Best Practices

  • Place headers in the first row
  • Make headers:
    • Bold
    • Slightly larger
    • Clearly labeled

Titles

If used:

  • Place above the data (not inside it)
  • Keep titles brief and descriptive

Do not mix data with titles or notes in the same rows.
