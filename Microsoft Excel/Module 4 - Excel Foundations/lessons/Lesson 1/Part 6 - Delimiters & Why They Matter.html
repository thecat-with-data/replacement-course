Lesson 1 Part 6: Delimiters & Why They Matter

Delimited files separate data using special characters.

Common Delimiters

  • Comma (,) — most common in CSV files
  • Tab
  • Semicolon

If the incorrect delimiter is chosen:

  • Data merges into one column
  • Column structure breaks

Excel import tools allow you to preview delimiter choices. Always verify before confirming an import.
