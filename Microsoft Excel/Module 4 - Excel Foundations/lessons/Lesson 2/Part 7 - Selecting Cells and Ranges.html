Part 7 - Selecting Cells and Ranges

Accurate selection is critical for formatting, sorting, and analysis.

Single Cell

  • Click once

Contiguous Range

  • Left-Click and drag
  • Or Shift + Left-Click

Entire Row or Column

  • Click row number or column letter

Non-Adjacent Selections

  • Hold Ctrl while selecting

Incorrect selection is one of the most common causes of Excel errors.
