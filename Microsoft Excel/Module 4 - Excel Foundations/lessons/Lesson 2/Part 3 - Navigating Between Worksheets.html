Part 4 - Navigating Between Worksheets

Worksheets are accessed via tabs at the bottom of the screen.

Basic Navigation

  • Click tabs to switch worksheets
  • Use arrows to scroll through tabs when many exist

Best Practices

  • Rename worksheets clearly (e.g. Raw Data, Summary, Sales 2025)
  • Avoid generic names like Sheet1 or Data

Well-named worksheets reduce confusion and support collaboration.
