Part 10 - Guided Practice (Before the Lab)

Before completing E-Lab 1, practice:

  1. Move between three worksheets
  2. Rename each worksheet meaningfully
  3. Use Ctrl + Arrow Keys to jump through data
  4. Use the Name Box to jump to a specific cell
  5. Select:
    • A single cell
    • A contiguous range
    • A full column
    • A non-adjacent range
