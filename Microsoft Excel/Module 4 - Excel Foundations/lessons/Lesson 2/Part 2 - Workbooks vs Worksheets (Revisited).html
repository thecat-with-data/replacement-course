Part 3 - Workbooks vs Worksheets (Revisited)

Understanding Excel's hierarchy is essential.

Workbook

  • The entire Excel file (.xlsx)
  • Can contain:
    • Multiple worksheets
    • Data, charts, and analysis

Worksheet

  • A single tab within a workbook
  • Contains rows, columns, and cells
  • Usually focused on a specific dataset or purpose

A single project may involve many worksheets within one workbook.
